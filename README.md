

# 🛡️ ShopShield AI

**Shop Smart || Shop Safe || Powered by AI.**

ShopShield AI is a secure eCommerce platform that helps users discover products and identify potentially unsafe shopping websites before making purchases.

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Many online shoppers face problems such as:

* Fake shopping websites
* Online scams
* Difficulty finding trustworthy products
* Lack of security awareness while shopping online

Users often cannot easily determine whether a shopping website is safe or suspicious.

## 💡 Solution

ShopShield AI combines online shopping with cyber security awareness.

* The platform allows users to browse products
* receive recommendations
* check website safety
* and get guidance through an AI assistant.

## ✨ MVP Features

### 🛍️ Product Listing

Browse available products with basic details.

### 🔍 Smart Product Search

Search products quickly using keywords.

### 🛡️ Website Safety Checker

Enter a shopping website URL and receive a safety status:

* Safe ✅
* Suspicious ⚠️
* Dangerous ❌

### 🤖 AI Product Recommendation

Get product suggestions based on selected categories.

### 💬 AI Shopping Assistant

Ask shopping-related questions and receive guidance.
